Crystal Craft

Welcome to Crystal Craft — a multi-mode Minecraft server where creativity, skill, and teamwork come together. Dive into classic survival, thrilling BedWars battles, or explore our unique custom survival world enhanced with datapacks.

About the Server

Crystal Craft offers a variety of game modes designed for every type of player:

  • BedWars: Battle other teams, defend your bed, and conquer the arena.
  • Survival: Classic survival with a friendly and active community.
  • Custom Survival: A datapack-powered experience with new mechanics, mobs, and progression.

Version: 1.21+
Type: Multi-Mode Server
Features: BedWars arenas, datapacks, community events, economy system, and much more!
